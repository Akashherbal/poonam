Heart Frayer Model

  • Term: Heart
  • Definition: The muscular organ in humans and animals that pumps blood through the circulatory system.
  • Characteristics:
    • Composed of cardiac muscle tissue
    • Four chambers: left atrium, left ventricle, right atrium, and right ventricle
    • Responsible for circulating oxygenated and deoxygenated blood
  • Examples:
    • Human heart
    • Animal hearts (e.g., dog heart, cat heart)
  • Non-Examples:
    • Lungs
    • Stomach
